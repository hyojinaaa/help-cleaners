Help and Learning for Cleaners

Get Started Our Delicious Tutorials and Become a Specialist in a Few Minutes!

1. Follow your invite link, Create an account, Download the app

Follow the link you received via SMS or email and click “Accept Invite.” Fill out your details to create an account. Then download the app on your smartphone. When you log in, remember to enter the account details you used when creating the account. Fill out your profile by adding a profile picture and set your preferred language.

The only way to start using Properly as a cleaner or housekeeper is to have your client(s) send you jobs.

Congratulations!
You finished the first step!

2. View Jobs Clients Send You

The Properly app is designed for you to receive checklists and use them while you work on your client’s property. You can view all of your upcoming jobs (jobs that have not already passed) in the tab Upcoming and all of the jobs you’ve completed in History.

3. Accept a Job

When a client sends you a new job request, you can find it in Upcoming. Open the job to see details such as date, time, location, property details, and a message if your client added one. You can preview the checklist to get a sense of your clients’ expectations before you accept it.

4. Start A Job, Use the checklist

When you’re at your job’s property, find the job in Upcoming. Open the job and tap Start Job. This action notifies your client that your session has begun and is now in progress.

Tap the task pins and swipe through the photos as you work through the checklist. Some pins will have extra instructions inside them. When you tap on those pins, the notes will appear. After you have read a note or simply tap a pin without a note, the pin will turn gray with a check mark. This indicates that it is completed.

5. Best Practices & General Info video

Overview is a great way to preview the entire list to see how many tasks you have left and how many you have checked off.

Whenever you want to pause your job, reset all your tasks, or manually finish the job tap "Done".
